About

Hi there!

I am a graduate student working in seismology and computational geophysics at IIT Bombay. My current work focuses on receiver functions, seismic waveform processing, and Bayesian inversion methods for understanding subsurface velocity structure.

I use Python, shell scripting, SAC/GSAC workflows, GMT, and numerical forward modelling tools to process earthquake waveform data, generate synthetic receiver functions, and visualize inversion results.

Research Interests

Receiver functions

Estimating crustal and upper-mantle structure using P-to-S converted phases, free-surface transformations, and waveform modelling.

Bayesian inversion

Using MCMC-based approaches to infer shear-wave velocity profiles and quantify model uncertainty.
